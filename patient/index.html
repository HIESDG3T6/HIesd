About Us

End to End solution for your clinic needs

Our mission is Health is Wealth. We aim to be your one-stop solution for anything to do with checking your symptoms to finding a clinic and booking appointments. We want your experience to be as painless as possible cause who needs more pain when they're already sick?

Services we offer include: Symptom Checking, Clinic Searching, Booking Appointments, Checking Previous Medical Histories

What we do

Symptom Checker

We provide online symptom checking to diagnose your illnesses and direct you to the correct specialists.

Search for Panel Clinics

We provide a one-stop place for you to search for panel clinics by name, location and specialty. We also provide the distance away from the clinics from where you are.

Booking Appointments

Book your appointments with our panel of clinics in a few clicks of a button.

Email and Telegram Notifications

Get notified about your appointments via Email and Telegram.

Directions to the Clinic

Get directions to the clinic you have an appointment with via our website.

Checking Your Appointment History

Forgotten what medicine was previously prescribed? Not to worry as we record these details for you.
